Quick Snacks For Summer Day

Ice cream Sandwich

This is a simple snack for when it gets hot outside and you need something sweet to cool you down.

Estimated time: 15 min.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Ice Creams flavor of your choice
  • Chocolate Chip Cookies

Directions:

  • Get two chocolate chip cookies and your choice of ice cream.
  • Set cookies on table and spread ice cream on to both cookies.Use as you like.
  • After spreading ice cream onto both cookies take both cookies and mash them together to make a sandwich
  • Sit the sandwich(es) in the freezer until ice cream is solid and enjoy!

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Estimated time:5min.

A quick toasted sandwich when you need something delicious to eat on the go!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Directions:

  • Get to slices of bread and put it in the toaster.
  • Spread peanut butter on one slice and Jelly on the other and put the slices together.Yes,its that quick and easy. Enjoy!
